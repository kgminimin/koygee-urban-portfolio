About Koy

I’m an amateur photographer with a growing passion for capturing the pulse of the city. I’m always seeking a challenge, drawn to new places, new light, and the moments that often go unseen.

Photography started as a curiosity and quickly became a way to slow down and really look — to find meaning in movement, people, and shadows. Whether it’s neon reflections at night or the quiet rhythm of a street at dawn, I love chasing those small details that make a scene feel alive.
